About
Created & Maintained by Matt Hanson

I am a local paraglider pilot flying since 2014. Wasatch Wind was created to quickly view the most prominent and pertinent local wind and weather infomation in one place to assist flying conditions assessment. The colors, themes, and details depicted here are generalized and not intended to influence a decision to fly.
