Profile



Hi! I am Matt Hanson, an avid paraglider pilot since 2014 based in Salt Lake City, UT. I'm also a hobbyist software developer and created this resource for fun and for assessing potential paragliding conditions in the Wasatch.

I enjoy writing and have contributed a few articles on the Wasatch Free Flight Group page, which is a great resource for local pilots. I hope to see you around - hopefully somewhere in the sky!
