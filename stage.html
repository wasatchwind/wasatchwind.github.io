Hello!

I'm Matt Hanson, an avid paraglider pilot based in Salt Lake City, UT. As a student of wind, weather, and technology, I created Wasatch Wind to better understand local flying conditions and to group resources in one convenient place.

Paragliding is an inherently dangerous activity, and Wasatch Wind is not a tool to calculate whether or not it is safe to fly. The choice to launch is yours and yours alone!
